Catering

At Monsoon Thunder Bay we specialize in catering.  Whether you have a group of 10 or a group of 100, we guarantee to provide exceptional food that is fresh and authentic.  Our staff and head chef are experienced in the area of catering.  As a result, our team understands how to provide a high level of quality and service that is customized to the needs of each customer that we work with.  In terms of catering, we offer the following services:

  • Ability to cater to various group sizes
  • Ability to work on-site cooking fresh food
  • Customized pricing – we offer discount pricing to larger catering groups
  • One on one support and meeting with our team and Head Chef to assure that the menu is catered to the needs of the group we are servicing
  • Follow up communication with our team to assure that all needs have been meet and exceeded
